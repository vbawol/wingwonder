Instructions for Use

  1. Hook the smaller end into the groove of the window channel.
  2. Secure the larger end by clipping it to the edge of the window.
  3. Adjust as needed: You can easily slide the Wing Wonder up or down to fine-tune the angle of the window for optimal airflow.
